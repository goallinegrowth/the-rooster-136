Our Story

A Little Place With
a Whole Lot of Heart

Nestled in a charming historic building in downtown Hartsville, The Rooster One Thirty Six has been serving the community since 2005. We believe in honest food — locally sourced, thoughtfully prepared, and served with genuine warmth.

From our handcrafted crepes to pimento cheese biscuits, every dish celebrates the flavors of the South with a fresh, modern twist. Pull up a chair in our eclectic dining room or out in the courtyard — you're always welcome here.
